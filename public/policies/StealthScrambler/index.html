Privacy Policy – Stealth Scrambler

Last updated: 1 January 2026

Overview

Stealth Scrambler is designed with privacy as a core principle. All processing is performed locally on your device. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored externally.

Data Collection

Stealth Scrambler does not collect any personal information. No analytics, tracking data, or identifiers are gathered.

Data Processing

All text entered into Stealth Scrambler is processed entirely on-device. No data is sent to servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

Internet Access

Stealth Scrambler does not require an internet connection to function.

Third-Party Services

Stealth Scrambler does not use advertising SDKs, analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party data processors.

Children’s Privacy

Stealth Scrambler does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. As no data is collected, there is no risk of personal information being stored.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the contact details listed on the Google Play Store page.
