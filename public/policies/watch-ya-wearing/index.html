Scope

This policy explains how the Android app Watch ya Wearing handles information.

Permissions

The app may request access to your device camera and photo library to allow you to add images of watches to your collection. These images are stored locally on your device unless you choose to back them up using your device or cloud provider.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information. Data remains on your device unless you back it up via your OS/cloud provider.

Data retention & deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through your device settings.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
